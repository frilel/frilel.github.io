Bio

Christian is a postgraduate student in Stockholm, Sweden, studying for a master's degree planned to finish in Summer 2023.

His passion lies in Computer Game Development and Design. With other areas of interest including Human-Computer Interaction, User Experience, User Research, Academic Research and Human Behavior. He enjoys working with design challenges with a user-centric approach. Researching the issue, designing and evaluating a solution with users. He works well both in groups and independently. He's also comfortable leading smaller teams as a senior within a field with which he has some experience of both professionally and academically.

Christian graduated with a BS in Computer- and Data Science from Stockholm University with a major in digital game development.

Before his bachelor studies he worked for 10 years as a full-time soldier and vehicle mechanic for the Swedish Armed Forces. As the work was not intellectually challenging enough, Christian resumed his earlier studies in Computer Science and Game Development in 2020, graduating in 2021.
