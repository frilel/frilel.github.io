Hello!

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Christian, a passionate nerdy gamer with a love for all things fantasy and science. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer & Systems Science specializing in Game Development, and a Master of Science degree in Computer Science specializing in Interactive Media Technology. I currently work with R&D of combat flight simulation at the Swedish Defence Research Agency.
